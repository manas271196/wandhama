• A nightmare revisited

    Reliving the Wandhama massacre of 1998.

  • نمونہ متن Wandhama is a quiet village in central Kashmir’s Ganderbal district. The name in Kashmiri means ‘hot in winter’ i.e. providing comfort to its people.

  • نمونہ متن But on 25th January 1998, Wandhama failed to live upto it's name. It witnessed the killing of 23 Kashmiri Hindus in the dead of the night by unidentified gunmen.

  • نمونہ متن The events of that night are recalled by one of the survivors of the incident, a 14-year-old Hindu boy named Vinod Kuman Dhar alias Ashu. The teenager had slipped away and hidden himself in a dark corner of the house.

  • نمونہ متن “The gunmen were dressed like Indian Army soldiers,”, Ashu said piecing the details together. “They had tea with us, waiting for a radio message indicating that all pandit families in the village had been covered.”

  • نمونہ متن After a brief conversation they rounded up all the members of the Hindu households and then gunned them down with Kalashnikov rifles.

  • نمونہ متن There was burning smell of the human flesh everywhere. A mother had unsuccessfully tried to save her infant by hiding him in her lap but both got killed together. The cop trying to separate them couldn’t.

  • These are their stories. The neighbourhood had changed forever. The victims included 10 men, 9 women and 4 children. These are their stories.
